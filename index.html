Josh Lunger About

My name is Josh Lunger (he/him) and I am a computational neuroscience researcher in the Linderman Lab at Stanford University. Previously I studied computer science and mathematics at the University of Toronto while working in the Cunningham Lab and Rajan Lab at Harvard Medical School. I believe that neural time-series is the language of the mind. My research focuses on scalable machine learning methods for high-dimensional brain data.
